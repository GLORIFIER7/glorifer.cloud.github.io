About GLORFIER SERVICES

    GLORIFIER SERVICES is Commited to United Nations SDG Advocacy and Outreach, seeks to put the power of communications behind the Sustainable Development Goals, also known as the Global Goals. We ‘make’ campaign materials, unique installations, ground-breaking documentaries, one of a kind events and as much impact as possible. We are a not-for-profit communications agency made up of a dedicated team of campaigners and communicators, who sit at the heart of a global network of civil society organisations, private sector partners, brands, governments, and UN agencies. The 17 Global Goals are at the core of everything we do – driving awareness and adoption – in order to create a world where extreme poverty is eradicated, climate change is properly addressed, and injustice and inequality are unacceptable.
